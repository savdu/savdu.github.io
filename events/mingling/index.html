what's there to do around here?

🎲 TAKE A CHANCE
Grab a chance card from the kitchen stack. (Look out! The hosts might serve you one at any moment.)

🍷 FEELING PARCHED?
Head to the kitchen and grab a drink for yourself and someone new.

☺️ FEELING SHY?
Find a host for assisted introductions.

👀 FEELING CREATIVE?
Test each others' artistic abilities with the art supplies on the kitchen table. Set a 5 minute timer and draw each other. Take this chance to look deep into each others' eyes.

❤️ FEELING A CONNECTION?
Find the candlelit picnic blankets by the back desk or in Sav's hallway.

📞 FEELING RISKY?
Grab a name & number from the hats near the entryway to set up a blind date.
